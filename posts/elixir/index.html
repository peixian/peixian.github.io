    • @counter -> Adds a prometheus metric
      • https://hexdocs.pm/prometheus_ex/Prometheus.Metric.html
      • Usage
        • declare a counter with 
          @counter [name: :name,
          labels: [:label1, :label2],
          help: "#{__MODULE__} name"]
        • increment a counter with 
          Counter.inc([name: :name, labels: [label1, label2]])
