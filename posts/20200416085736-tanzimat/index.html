    tanzimat

    Tags: ottoman

    Political reforms aimed at appeasing European Great Powers for equality of citizenship and abolishment of the millet system

    • Announced in 1839
    • Promulgated in 1856
    • Reforms were run through a series of dragomans, described by Philliou: Interpreting the Ottoman Past through the National Present
    • Reforms attemped to centralize all currencies into a single treasury issued currency
    • Streamlining the Ottoman empire’s coercive abilites to command economic production - Blumi - Ottoman Refugees, 1878-1939
