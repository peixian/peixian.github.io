    NGO’s as newsmakers

    • Journalists cite NGO’s, NGO’s cite the prestige at being in the “paper of record”, which creates an iron feedback loop
    • NGO’s hire workers, although the fault here is the danger of NGO intentions
    • “news is where the journalists are” -> “news is where the NGO’s are”
      • good for both, it’s easier for work on them
    • NGO’s are inherently political, which allows smuggling of politics into news
      • is there objective news though?
    • NGO donor base profits from this as well
    • Binding of advocacy, fund raising, and NGO’s
      • the NGO existence is premised on the hollowing of states
      • english news donates towards english NGO’s
      • all of this is fair game, but creates a warped view
    • what gets left on the cutting room floor?
