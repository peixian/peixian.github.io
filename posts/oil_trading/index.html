    Ticker Strategy

    Futures

    Regular Futures

    • “Root Ticker” + “Month” + “Year”
    • Example: “CL” + “Z” + “2” = CLZ2, (WTI crude) expiring December 2022

    Active Tickers

    • “Root Ticker” + “A”
    • Example: “CLA” - WTI active
    • Best gauge of market sentiment

    Generic or Rolling Tickers

    • “Root Ticker” + “1”
    • “CL1” - Front of the month
