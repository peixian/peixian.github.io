    ooda (observe orient decide act)

    Tags: episteme

    • https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OODA%5Floop

    Called the OODA loop, prioritizes agility over raw power by increasing the tempo of decisions

    • If decisions are made faster, then you can then observe the adversary’s OODA loop
    • Eventually aiming for the breakdown of the adversary’s ooda loop by exploting weaknesses
