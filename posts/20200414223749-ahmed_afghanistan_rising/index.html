    CUP officiers arrived in early years of Afghanistan under Aman Allah, where they played a major role in early “moderization”.

    Presents a very rosy Pan-Islamic view of the CUP officiers.

    Aman Allah

    • Greatly respected mustafa kemal
    • Configured the early Afghan state
    • Multiple leaps in early jurisprudence in afghanistan, such as:
      • oversight of a constitutional draft committee comprised of jurists and theorists and members of the ummah
      • All laws drafted under the first constitutional committee required a match with sharia law before passing to Aman Allah
    • Later years hampered by aggressive spending, eventually ousted in 1928
